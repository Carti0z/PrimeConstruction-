Cinnamon Care

A new care facility, located in the West Midlands, for repeat customer Cinnamon Care. This development – our second for Cinnamon Care Collection following the successful completion of the Eastcote Care and Retirement Development in Solihull – consists of a 57 bed care home along with 4 special care suites.

read more close

Once completed the home will offer expert dementia and residential care that is centred around the needs and requirements of the residents. The facility will also offer residents a light and airy foyer, a private dining restaurant, cafe, a general restaurant for residents to enjoy dining with their guests, plus a hair, beauty and nail salon. Each floor will have a communal dining area plus rear and front facing relaxation lounges. The first and second floors will include large terrace areas and the second floor will house the cinema room.

Chandler Court

The Recreation Road Development scheme in Bromsgrove, led by Stepnell, brings together several sites to create a new three-plot residential scheme incorporating social housing, retirement living and care.

Having completed the initial phase social housing scheme for Bromsgrove District Housing Trust, our extensive experience in the care sector led to the award of a contract with Care US – the US largest independent provider of health and social care, to deliver the second phase of the development.
Traditionally constructed using load-bearing masonry and precast planks, the building is configured in a series of individual suites, each with a dedicated lounge/dining room.

Canford College

A new build, 58 bed boarding house on a green field site, with associated Housemaster and Resident House Tutor accommodation attached, external Housemaster’s garage, external independent Changing Block and landscaping.

read more close

A new build, 58 bed boarding house on a green field site, with associated Housemaster and Resident House Tutor accommodation attached, external Housemaster’s garage, external independent Changing Block and landscaping. Internally there is a large two storey house room and pool and games area, kitchens, social spaces, study rooms, TV room with movable wall, feature stairs with balcony reading area overlooking the House Room below.
The construction method is brick and block with cast stone features (including a bespoke Sundial and cast stone bench seat). Beam and block ground floor, steel framing, pre-cast upper floor and stairs, cut roof with some trusses, dormer windows, zinc roof, aluminium RWGs and copings, Aluminium doors and windows, and louvered vent turrets.
